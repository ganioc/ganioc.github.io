What a normal programmer could do? Mostly they are information , such as images, bytes, bits, manipulators, they are not able to manipulate the hardware. They are building servers, websites, apps, games, social-media things, by hitting the keyboards in front of LCD screens. They can't use hardware level programming language to manipulate the hardware. To do with hardware , you need a voltmeter, an oscilloscope, to measure it and test it, debugging the program. That's not something normal programmers do today. The question is the hardware is not so smart for programmers to do their magic before a laptop computer.

## Who can help those programmers?
A script language to help programmers to manipulate hardware, so they can use the smart hardware, and develop all kinds of applications for civilians.
